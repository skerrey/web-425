Welcome to the About Us Page

Overview

Loan_App is one of the leading financial loan calculators for the Slytherin House, students and otherwise. Created by Salazar Slytherin pre 976, Loan_App has been assisting students of the Slytherin House at Hogwarts with their finances for over 1000 years. Loan_App was originally used on magic parchment that Slytherin students were given as a part of their orientation week at Hogwarts, and initiation into Slytherin house. It has since been updated to a website by Lucius Malfoy, one of the many great benefactors of the Slytherin House. (The option to use Loan_App is still available on magic parchment but restricted to those of pure blood only).

Benefits

Loan_App comes with great wizardly functions like analyzing the magic of a financial loan. It includes inputs of loan amount, interest rate, and number of years. With these variables any wizard, pure blood or not, can benefit from Loan_App’s magical calculations. Students will be able to calculate their loans for interests such as: the fastest flying brooms that are offered, the best wands from Olivander’s Wands, and any magical pet they desire. Students can even use Loan_App for smaller loans if they need to calculate how much to borrow for Weasley’s Wizard Wheezes candy shop!

History

The history of Loan_App includes being used by the great Salazar Slytherin as he could not figure out how much of a loan he needed for his magical pet. He wanted a Hungarian Horntail but he wasn’t sure of his budget. He ended up getting a Basilisk, and not long after created Loan_App so students could calculate their financial loans properly to get the pets they desire.
