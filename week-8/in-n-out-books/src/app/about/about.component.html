Who is James Patterson?

James Brendan Patterson is an American Author. He is best known for his series: Alex Cross, Michael Bennet, Maximum Ride, and Private series. His genre of writing is mystery, fiction, and thriller. Patterson has sold more than 400 million copies of his books and was the first person to sell 1 million e-books. In 2015, Patterson received the Literarian Award from the National Book Foundation which described him as a “passionate campaigner to make books and reading a national priority.”
